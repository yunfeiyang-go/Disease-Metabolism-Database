Get started with D&M

      D&M(Disease and Metabolic Information Database), a comprehensive database which integrated HMDB and OMIM and KEGG, where you are able to look up disease names to get the corresponding genetic information and metabolic information, and also avaliable for looking up metabolite names to get the corresponding disease information and metabolic information. For some diseases, D&M also provides metabolic pathway maps drawn by Cytoscape.js to see all disease-related metabolites and interactions at a glance.
